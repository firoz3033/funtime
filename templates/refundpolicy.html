Refund Policy

At Fun Time, we strive to ensure that our customers are satisfied with our services. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Refunds

We offer a full refund within 30 days of your purchase. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we cannot offer you a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • The service must not have been fully delivered or completed.
  • You must provide proof of purchase.
  • Any additional conditions specific to the service purchased (if any) must be met.

Refund Process

To initiate a refund, please contact us at support@funtime.com with your order details and the reason for the refund request. Our team will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days.

If your refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within a certain number of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.

Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at support@funtime.com.

Non-Refundable Services

Some services may not be eligible for a refund. These include:

  • Services that have been fully delivered or completed.
  • Custom services tailored specifically to your requirements.
  • Other conditions that will be specified at the time of purchase.

Changes to This Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
